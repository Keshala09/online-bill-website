ABOUT US

- PAYBILL About Us -


- WHO WE ARE -


We are currently the best and largest bill payment platform in Sri Lanka. You can pay the bill using any payment method by visiting our website and we can also help you remind you of missed or forgotten payments. Once you have entered the payment details we will make sure to remind you weekly, monthly or annually as you wish.We provide various bill payments and their reminders. Also we provide make events and their reminders.



- OUR VISION -


With our innovative technology, we would like to see users grow their businesses every year and facilitate their day-to-day transactions. We are committed to making your payments more efficient, flexible and less time consuming. We also hope to help you live a better life by reminding you of the payments you make in your busy life. That is our vision.



- OUR MISSION -


PAYBILL strives for an optimally effective, secure, reliable, accessible and socially efficient payment system. To this end, it performs public interest services such as electricity, water, telephone bills (collective functions) and manages those collective functions. It also handles money securely during payments and protects the confidentiality of credit, debit or others card information. Transparency, openness, accessibility are important preconditions for the successful performance of our tasks.
